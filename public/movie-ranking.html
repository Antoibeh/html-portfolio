The Best Movies According to Anthony C. Ibeh

My top three movies of all-time.

FROM

Boyd Stevens (Harold Perrineau) is the sheriff, and each day at dusk, he rings a bell to warn residents to get inside and lock their doors.

Let Go

A jaded mother makes a last-ditch effort to keep her family together by taking them on a trip to their teenage daughter's pole dancing competition.

Rebel Ridge

A former Marine confronts corruption in a small town when local law enforcement unjustly seizes the bag of cash he needs to post his cousin's bail.
